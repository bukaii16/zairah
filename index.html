Luffy

Luffy possesses the power of the Gum-Gum Fruit,
which grants his body extraordinary elasticity, allowing him to
stretch and contort in superhuman ways. His personality is
marked by boundless optimism, a strong sense of justice,
unwavering loyalty to his friends. Throughout the series,
he builds a diverse crew known as the Straw Hat Pirates and
embarks on numerous adventures across the Grand Line.
